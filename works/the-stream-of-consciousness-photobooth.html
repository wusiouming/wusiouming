The Stream of Consciousness Photobooth

Interactive Generative Installation • 2025

Concept

The Stream of Consciousness Photobooth is an interactive generative installation that integrates real-time image capture, artificial intelligence language generation, and speech synthesis technology. In a playful and theatrical manner, this creation deconstructs and semantically transforms participants' facial images through algorithms into unique textual totems. Simultaneously, an AI model analyzes the captured images and generates a spoken monologue that simulates an individual's inner experience, resulting in an atypical photobooth outcome that unveils the user's stream of consciousness.

This artwork aims to explore the translation, representation, and potential alienation of individual consciousness under the intervention of digital media and artificial intelligence. By converting visual information into visible and audible textual forms, the piece questions the construction and representation of individual identity in the digital age. It also reflects on the tension between the algorithm's \"objectivity\" and subjective individual experiences, as well as the impact of the alienation of subjectivity and self-awareness in a context where AI increasingly permeates daily life. Furthermore, the work borrows the medium of popular culture to guide viewers in contemplating the power and limitations of language as a symbolic system in capturing and conveying individual experiences, and the trends of commodification and externalization of inner experiences in the digital era.
