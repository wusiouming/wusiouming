A Stage for Everyone

Interactive Audiovisual Installation • 2016

Concept

"A Stage for Everyone" is an interactive installation that transforms light and sound into an immersive environment. The work operates through real-time sound detection, generating dynamic light patterns and minimalist musical layers in response to the audience's auditory input.

The installation welcomes participants to interact in any way they choose—whether through singing, playing instruments, or simply talking. As sound is detected, the colors and composition of the music shift accordingly. By lowering the threshold of interaction to basic vocalization, the work encourages individuals from all backgrounds to present themselves and claim their presence on this digital stage.
