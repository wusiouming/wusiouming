The Birds that Leave the Nest

Interactive Sound Installation • 2023

Concept

The Birds that Leave the Nest is an interactive sound sculpture developed from the artist’s interest in the hidden life of birds and the fragile threshold between presence and absence. Composed of electronic parts, sensors, acrylic, wood chips, and feathers, the work builds a nest-like form that invites the viewer to touch, search, and listen.

The circular structure conceals sensors inside, and the changing light conditions in the exhibition space modulate the sounds of birds. When the audience reaches into the device to look for the birds, the increased light activates a response: the chirping fades away. The work turns listening into a game of approach and withdrawal, allowing visitors to imagine themselves as birds seeking companions within the nest.
