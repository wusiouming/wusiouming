The English Word “Asia”

Video • Sound • 2015

Video Documentation

Watch video documentation →

Concept

I searched the English Wikipedia page for the word “Asia,” extracted fragments of text, and pasted them into Google Translate. I then pressed “listen” while selecting Chinese as the output. The resulting sound becomes a strange hybrid: it resembles Chinese but is spoken with an English tone, producing a ridiculous language. The video uses footage of U.S. warplanes in the western Pacific during World War II, captured from Internet Archive and rearranged. The work asks: who produced the concept of Asia?
