Rainbow Stage

Interactive Audiovisual Installation • 2020

Concept

Rainbow Stage is an interactive visual and sound installation, an extension of the artist's project 'Color Lab.' The project is based on the diverse vocal expressions of human beings, transforming individual characteristics into color expressions through personal voice data. This installation also uses sound as a medium to symbolize individual differences and personalities. By detecting the live audience's voices through a microphone, the device analyzes voice data—including frequency, amplitude, and period—and maps them into various color compositions. Over time, the installation continuously forms a collective color image, creating a vibrant visual symphony akin to a rainbow, symbolizing the interaction of diverse cultures and human groups.

A Symphony of Sound and Color

Rainbow Stage' is an interactive light and sound work that transforms your voice into a unique color performance. Continuing the concept of the artist's 'Color Lab,' the piece utilizes programming and sound analysis technology to capture each audience member's voice data in real-time. These personalized vocal traits are translated into exclusive colors presented within the space. Rainbow Stage is not just a space to showcase individual colors, but a field that celebrates diversity and affirms individual differences. Everyone's voice is unique; when they intertwine and collage on stage to form a magnificent color image, we see the beauty of diverse individuals coexisting in harmony.

How to Interact

We believe that everyone's voice has its own unique color. Now, we invite you to say something into the microphone—speaking louder often produces more vibrant effects!
